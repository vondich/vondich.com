About Me

Hi, I'm Nin Dichoso, a Software Engineer from Manila. I started as a Visual Basic developer back in 2007 right after I finished my degree in Computer Science. In 2008, I decided to shift to Web Development and ever since I've been developing web applications using PHP. In 2011, I taught myself Android and developed Android applications for the company I was working for at that time. In 2012, I moved to Singapore to work for Gemalto as a Software Engineer. Currently, I am a Senior Backend Engineer at CarPal Singapore.

Aside from software development, I also enjoy taking photos and binge-watching my favorite TV series (thanks to Netflix!). I am always looking for new music to listen to and like most geeks, I'm addicted to new technology.
